PRICES

  • FULL PRICE: €7
  • REDUCED PRICE: €4 (school pupils, students, jobseekers, pensioners)
  • FREE for: under-26s, MDPH cardholders (+ 1 accompanying person), members of PSL University (students and staff), members of Mines Télécom (students and staff), ICOM cardholders, ABC Mines members.

    • Please note: with a full-price ticket for one of the three Paris Mineralogy collections (“Treasures of the Earth” at the National Museum of Natural History in the Jardin des Plantes, “Mineral Collection” on the Pierre and Marie Curie campus, “Mineralogy Museum” at Mines Paris - PSL), you are entitled to a REDUCED RATE at the other two collections. Keep your ticket safe – it has no expiry date!
