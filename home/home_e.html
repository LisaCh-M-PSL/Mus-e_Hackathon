Welcome to the Mineralogical Museum of the Mines de Paris!


For over 200 years, housed within the Hôtel de Vendôme, the mineralogical collections of Mines Paris - PSL have been counted amongst the most comprehensive and spectacular in the world. They contain some 100,000 specimens, 5,000 of which are on display, representing around 2,900 mineral species!

Whilst it takes just a few seconds to be struck by the beauty and diversity of these mineral treasures, decades of study have been required to understand their inner nature. For this museum is not merely a repository of species and a showcase for the richness of the mineral world; it is also a centre for research.

In particular, the growing use of rare chemical elements (lanthanides, tantalum, germanium, etc.) in new technologies makes it necessary to analyse the mineral databases compiled over the centuries. This analysis informs strategies for the exploitation of necessarily limited resources.

By exploring our collections of minerals, rocks, meteorites and gemstones, you will be able to appreciate the various facets of their history, revealing their full beauty and utility.
